Rules

Deckbuilding

Each player brings a 40-card deck built around lane pressure and signal timing.

Turn Flow

At the start of your turn, draw 1 card, commit units, then resolve attacks from left to right.

Combat Resolution

When a lane battle starts, attackers and defenders lock in at the same time. Both sides deal damage at the same time unless a signal changes the exchange.

Momentum Response Window

After assignments are set, both players get one short response window to bank shield charge or punish an overcommit before damage lands.
