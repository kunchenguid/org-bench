Chronicle Clash

A fast deck dueling format where every turn asks whether to deploy, reinforce, or cash in momentum for a finishing push.

Setup

Each player shuffles a 30-card deck, draws five cards, and starts at 20 life. Flip a coin to decide who takes the opening turn.

Turn Sequence

  1. Refresh exhausted cards and draw one card.
  2. Play one unit and any number of tactics you can afford.
  3. Attack with ready units in any order.
  4. End the turn and discard down to seven cards.

Combat Resolution

When a unit attacks, the defender chooses whether to intercept with one ready unit or take the hit directly. Both units deal damage at the same time unless a tactic says otherwise.

Momentum windows let defenders blunt an attack or punish an overcommit.

Deckbuilding Rules

  • Decks contain exactly 30 cards.
  • No more than three copies of the same card name.
  • Choose one faction and keep at least 24 cards in-faction.

Win Condition

You win immediately when your opponent reaches 0 life or must draw from an empty deck.
