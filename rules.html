Rules

DivB's rules page focuses on a clear first read: build a legal deck, play a clean turn, and understand how a match ends.

Deckbuilding

Bring a 40-card deck. You can include up to 3 copies of any card with the same name unless that card says otherwise.

Shuffle your deck before each game and draw 5 cards for your opening hand.

Turn Flow

At the start of your turn, draw 1 card, ready your exhausted cards, and resolve any effects that trigger at turn start.

During your main step, play cards, attack, and use abilities in any order allowed by card text.
