Board State

Three lanes define the fight: left protects your draw engine, center is the fastest damage line, and right is the safest reinforcement lane.

Left Lane - stable blockers and value units.
Center Lane - direct pressure and tempo swings.
Right Lane - setup space for your next reinforce turn.

Target Priority

The AI strikes the lane with the weakest blocker first, then converts overflow into face pressure.
